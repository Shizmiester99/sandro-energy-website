Why Choose Us

At Sandro Energy, we proudly supply worldclass electrical products from Sieyuan Electric Co., Ltd., a globally respected leader in power transmission and distribution equipment. Founded over 30 years ago, Sieyuan has a long history of innovation and excellence in electrical technology, backed by extensive R&D and manufacturing capabilities. Sieyuan Their products are used in power grids, renewable energy, industrial systems, and major infrastructure projects across more than 100 countries worldwide, including throughout Europe where they support utilities and national grids.

The Sandro Advantage:

Exclusive Access to the Comprehensive Sieyuan Product Range: Sieyuan’s portfolio includes highvoltage switchgear, GIS, transformers (oilimmersed and gasinsulated), circuit breakers up to 500 kV, instrument transformers, reactive power compensation devices, power electronics, and smart grid equipment — enabling complete and integrated power solutions.

Global Reach & Recognition: Sieyuan’s equipment meets Australian and  international standards and has been supplied to key utilities in Europe, including the UK, Italy, Spain, the Netherlands, and other national grids, reflecting trust and quality at a global level.

Innovation & Quality: With strong partnerships (such as the SieyuanToshiba transformer joint venture) and advanced manufacturing facilities, products are engineered for reliability, efficiency, and longterm performance in demanding environments.

Tailored Solutions: Sieyuan’s flexible approach allows for customised specifications to meet local and industryspecific requirements — and we ensure you get precisely the products that match your operations.

By choosing Sandro Energy, you are accessing premium products backed by decades of experience, global expertise, and a trusted reputation — ensuring your electrical infrastructure is efficient, safe, and futureready.
